This is the short story of Vinayak

Godess Gowri created Ganesha by making an idol of her desire and she gave life to the idol of turmeric and sandle,then maa gowri called ganesha as her son!!

Maa gowri called ganesha to watch and not to allow anyone inside her home as she went to take bathing,at the time shiva came and tried to enter in but ganesh wont allow him in!

Shiva got anger and he was not aware that he is the son of parvati,even ganesh wont aware that he is his father, finally shiva cutted ganesha's head with shoola

Maa gowri came out side and saw the disaster of her son, she got frustrated and ware a demon form and started destroying nature inthe greef of her sons death

Lord shiva understands the crisis and orderd nandi to get an elephant head which will be sleeping in the north direction,nandi brought it and shiva joined elephant head to ganesha and gave life to him.

one day there was a fight between ganesha and subramanya for golden mango , then mahadev put a condition to resolve this fight "one who round the world and return here first they will get the mango"

subramanya went roaming the world and on his peacock,but ganesha with his wisdom he auumed his parents as world and made a round them, after the arrival of his brother shiva declares ganesha as winner

Once Veda vyasa went to kailasa in-search of writer who can write the things faster as he says,He asked shiva to send ganesha to write Vedopanishats and The Epic Mahabarath

Once Ganesha was roaming on his vechical mouse , he slipped out and fall on ground, at the time moon laughs and critisize ganesh,Thus Ganesha curse moon to loss his shine and to disappear.

thank you all for reading this may your life will be blessed by ganesha and stay safe ,stay happy, HAPPY GANESH CHATURTI
